From a Passion to South India's Most Trusted BGMI Marketplace

Maddy BGMI Store's journey started in 2019 with the founding of Maddy Recovery Hub, recovering BGMI accounts and building a reputation of trust across South India.

What started as a small WhatsApp trading group has grown into a full-fledged platform serving thousands of players. Every transaction is handled personally, every account is verified, and every buyer walks away satisfied.

We believe in transparent pricing, safe logins, and honest communication — values that have kept us going strong for 7 years and counting.
